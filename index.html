About Me


I am a Nutrition and Food student and I work as a lab assistant at Ryerson University. I have always had a passion for learning about foods and health. It is incredible to know that nutrition plays a major part of our lives. Not only that foods help fuel activities, it also helps our body defend itself from diseases. Furthermore, it has the power to bring people together in social gathers and bring different cultures together.

To promote overall well-being, I have started to learn web development to begin building a platform to share fun, enjoyable, and healthy recipes for the general Canadian population. After building websites, it has deepened my understanding of coding. I am now a freelance web develpper who is contantly exploring web technology.
